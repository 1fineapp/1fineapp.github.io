About the App

Every person needs motivation. And motivation is needed in two main things: to do something and not to do something. The $1 Fine app gives users double motivation: setting a reward for a person for doing something and paying a fine if they don't.
For example, a person sets himself the goal of running 2 kilometers in the morning, clearly indicating in the application the name of the task, the date of completion, the award and the fine amount. If a person completed task, then he rewards himself, for example, a delicious breakfast or a shower, if doesn't or he gave up , then he pays a fine.
So, a person develops a habit, due to which it will be easier to do something useful (or, on the contrary, not to do something in an attempt to quit a bad habit) rather than not to do it. The influence of these two factors on human motivation has been scientifically proven over and over again.
The application will be useful for most people who are looking for motivation to change their lives for the better.
